# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated: February 2026**

Traver Hardwood Floors ("we," "us," or "our") operates the website traverhardwoodfloors.com. This privacy policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your choices regarding that information.

## Information We Collect

### Information You Provide

When you contact us through our website, we collect the information you submit in the contact form, which may include:

- Your name
- Email address
- Phone number
- The content of your message

We only collect this information when you voluntarily submit it through our contact form.

### Information Collected Automatically

We use **Plausible Analytics** to understand how visitors use our website. Plausible is a privacy-focused analytics tool that does not use cookies, does not collect personal data, and does not track individual visitors across sites. The data collected includes general information such as pages visited, referring websites, browser type, and approximate geographic region. This data is aggregated and cannot be used to identify you personally.

You can review Plausible's data practices at https://plausible.io/data-policy.

### Facebook Pixel

This website uses the **Meta (Facebook) Pixel**, a tracking tool that collects data about your activity on our site and sends it to Meta Platforms, Inc. The Pixel places cookies on your browser and may collect information including pages you visit, actions you take on the site, your IP address, browser information, and device identifiers. This data is used to measure the effectiveness of our advertising on Facebook and Instagram, and to show you relevant ads on those platforms.

The Facebook Pixel involves the transfer of data to Meta, which may use it according to its own data policy. You can learn more about how Meta uses your data at https://www.facebook.com/privacy/policy/.

**Your choices:**
- You can opt out of interest-based advertising from Facebook through your Facebook ad settings: https://www.facebook.com/settings/?tab=ads
- You can opt out of third-party cookies through the Digital Advertising Alliance: https://optout.aboutads.info/
- Most browsers allow you to block or delete cookies through their settings

## How We Use Your Information

We use the information you provide through our contact form to:

- Respond to your inquiry
- Provide estimates or quotes for hardwood flooring services
- Schedule consultations or appointments
- Communicate with you about flooring projects you've requested

We use data collected through the Facebook Pixel to:

- Measure the effectiveness of our advertising campaigns
- Show relevant advertisements to people who have visited our website
- Understand how visitors interact with our site after seeing our ads

## Information Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties.

We may share your information only in the following circumstances:

- **Service providers:** We may share information with tools and services that help us operate our website (such as our analytics provider, and Meta for advertising purposes), but only to the extent necessary for those services to function.
- **Legal requirements:** We may disclose information if required to do so by law or in response to a valid legal request.

## Cookies

This website uses cookies for the following purposes:

- **Facebook Pixel:** Places cookies to track site activity for advertising measurement and targeting on Meta platforms.

You can control or delete cookies through your browser settings. Disabling cookies may affect the functionality of some features on this site.

## Data Retention

Information submitted through our contact form is retained only as long as necessary to respond to your inquiry and provide the services you've requested. We do not maintain your personal information beyond what is needed for ongoing business communication.

## Your Rights

Depending on where you are located, you may have the right to:

- Request access to the personal information we hold about you
- Request that we correct or delete your personal information
- Opt out of any tracking or advertising cookies (see instructions above)

To exercise any of these rights, contact us using the information below.

## Children's Privacy

Our website is not directed at children under the age of 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children.
