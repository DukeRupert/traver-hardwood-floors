Meet Chris Traver

Chris Traver, Owner

As a sixth-generation Montana native from Bozeman, Chris brings 18 years of hardwood flooring expertise to every project. His passion for the craft began early, and he has since become one of the region's most respected flooring professionals.

Chris specializes in custom homes and historic floor restoration, having honed his skills at some of Montana's most prominent flooring firms before relocating to Helena in 2023.

Professional Excellence

Chris is recognized for his expertise in water-based finishes and modern techniques. His work has been featured in national wood flooring publications, and he's a proud recipient of the "Northwest Wood Floor of the Year" award.

Known for his creative approach, Chris transforms even severely deteriorated floors into stunning works of art. Whether you have a floor hidden under carpet or one damaged by time and wear, he sees the potential where others see problems.

A Family Legacy

Chris has mentored four sons in the trade, passing down the traditions of quality craftsmanship that have defined the Traver name. When you choose Traver Hardwood Floors, you're choosing a family committed to excellence in every board, every finish, and every detail.
